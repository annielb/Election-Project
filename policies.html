    • Here are some examples of each candidate's stance on current prevalent issues.

    Candidate Immigration Foreign Policy Guns Health Care Abortion Gay Marriage
    Hillary Clinton Wants to end family detention, close immigrant detention centers, and help more eligible people become citizens. Wants to maintain a good military, strengthen alliances, defeat ISIS, and enforce the Iran nuclear deal. Wants comprehensive background checks and to close loopholes that allow guns to fall into the wrong hands. Wants to defend the Affordable Care Act, improve it, and reduce its costs. Wants to stop drug companies from charging excessive prices and provide new credit to those with high health expenses. Is pro-choice; Will stand up for Planned Parenthood and provide women with access to safe, legal abortions. Supports Supreme Court's ruling on nationwide marriage equality; Will make sure LGBT+ Americans are equal and wants to end discrimination in employment, housing, etc.
    Donald Trump Wants to enforce immigration laws; Wants to build a border wall and end sanctuary cities, and deport criminal aliens. Wants to destroy radical Islamic terrorist groups and end the nuclear deal with Iran. Supports the right to bear arms. Wants to get serious about prosecuting criminals, and wants to empower law-abiding gun owners to defend themselves. Supports access to affordable, high quality health care, not just insurance. Wants to repeal Obamacare and create a patient-centered health care system; Wants to provide for the sale of health insurance across state lines. Pro-life except for under certain conditions such as rape, incest, and the life of the mother. Opposes the use of government funds to pay for abortions. Believes same-sex marriage was an issue that should have been decided by the states, not the federal government.
